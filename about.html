Life Beyond the Code

While my work revolves around LLMs , life outside of it is just as fascinating. I enjoy socializing occasionally—meeting new people, exchanging perspectives, and understanding how they perceive life. Psychology intrigues me, especially how it shapes decisions, interactions, and even the way we approach learning.

When it comes to hobbies, I have a competitive streak in table tennis and chess, and I keep myself active with jogging. But what excites me even more is the thrill of learning new things—skiing and surfing are on my list, and this year, I'm planning to try skydiving. The ultimate dream? One day, I want to learn how to fly an aircraft.
